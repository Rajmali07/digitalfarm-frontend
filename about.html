About Digital Farm

Digital Farm is a smart livestock biosecurity platform designed to connect awareness, farmer reporting, AI support, records, and admin action in one practical system.

Why We Built It

Built for real farm coordination

Many livestock problems are not caused only by a lack of information, but by delays between noticing symptoms, recording them, escalating issues, and responding at the admin level. Digital Farm was built to reduce that gap.

Public awareness to action Users can move from awareness content into support, feedback, or reporting without losing context.
Farmer-friendly workflows Daily records, complaints, biosecurity checks, and health tracking are structured to be simple and practical.
Admin visibility Zone monitoring, complaint handling, feedback review, and community communication stay connected.

Our Mission

Protect Farms

Reduce disease outbreaks through better biosecurity practices.

Empower Farmers

Provide simple tools for risk assessment and farm management.

Data-Driven Decisions

Enable smarter decisions using real-time analytics.

What We Do

Risk Assessment

Analyze farm data and calculate biosecurity risk score.

Real-Time Alerts

Notify farmers about disease outbreaks and risks.

Training & Awareness

Educate farmers on best biosecurity practices.

Compliance Tracking

Help farms follow government biosecurity standards.

Our Values

Practical support

We focus on tools and flows that are useful for real livestock operations, not just technical demos.

Prevention first

Connected decision-making

Better decisions happen when public guidance, farmer data, and admin review all support one another.
